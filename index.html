Customer Pick #1: Pink Passion

Pink Passion is a classic choice and once of the OG menu items here at Beach Bar. The refreshing taste of strawberries and raspberries mixed with a raspberry sherbet it will be sure to cool you down while the day is just heating up. Some add a classic energy drink to help them get up and going!

Customer Pick #2: Oceanside Orange

Oceanside Orange is a citrus smoothie that is perfect for sitting by the ocean, drinking a sweet smoothie. Orange Sherbet, Oranges, along with a hint of lemon smoothie is a go to for those enjoy a tangy refresher. Oceanside Orange doesn't need an ocean view to taste delicious, but it sure doesn't hurt! .
