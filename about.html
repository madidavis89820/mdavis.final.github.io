A Little About Us

Beach Bar Smoothie Cafe started when two friends decided they had a passion for great drinks and good times. A cold beverage is a must when wandering the beach and when that cold beverage has fresh fruit and a sweet tang there is not much better. Seeing the smiles on our customer's faces is nothing less than contagious and hearing the "ahh" after the first drink is music to our ears.
